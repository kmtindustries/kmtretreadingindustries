About K.M.T.

K.M.T. was established in 1995 in Narwana, District Jind, Haryana. The company began by manufacturing equipment for hot tyre retreading, along with the production of moulds and dies. With the growing adoption of cold retreading technology, K.M.T. developed its own range of automatic equipment to meet evolving market demands.

As the industry expanded, K.M.T. continuously elevated its standards through automation and regular upgrades of its machinery. We take pride in being the first machinery manufacturer from Narwana, Haryana to enter international markets. The company has extensive experience in supplying, installing, and servicing equipment for both State Transport Undertakings (STUs) and Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) across India.

We are committed to delivering high-performance equipment with unmatched efficiency. Backed by modern manufacturing and testing facilities, K.M.T. ensures complete customer satisfaction through reliable and dedicated after-sales support.
